## STEP 6 — WHAT TO DO WITH RESPONSES

Every response notification goes to your Gmail. Here's the manual workflow until you have
enough volume to automate:

1. Response comes in → Gmail notification
2. Check Question 11 (pricing comfort) first — this is your qualifier
3. If "Yes — $20/month works" → reach out within 24 hours via email or phone
4. If "I'd need to see the product first" → send them a quick demo walkthrough
5. Log every lead in your Google Sheet tracker (Name, Trade, Email, Phone, Date,
   Follow-up date, Status, Notes)
6. Follow up at least 3 times before marking as closed
